Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LockGram

What is LockGram and how does it work? +

LockGram is a Web3-powered social application that runs as a Telegram Mini-App. It combines privacy-first messaging with cryptocurrency rewards. You earn LockCoins for interactions, and can convert them to LOCK tokens. Everything runs through Telegram, so no separate app download is needed.

How do I earn LockCoins? +

You earn LockCoins through various activities: sending messages, creating posts and stories, inviting friends, and engaging with content. The more active you are, the more you earn. Quality content and active community participation earn higher rewards.

Is LockGram really secure and private? +

Yes, absolutely. LockGram uses end-to-end encryption, zero data collection policies, and decentralized blockchain architecture. We don't store your messages, personal data, or activity logs. Regular third-party security audits ensure maximum protection.

Do I need to install anything? +

No installation required! LockGram is a Telegram Mini-App, which means it runs directly within Telegram. Simply click "Launch Mini-App" and you'll be redirected to LockGram within your Telegram app. Works on mobile, desktop, and web versions of Telegram.

What are LOCK tokens and how do they work? +

LOCK is the main cryptocurrency token of the LockGram ecosystem. You can convert your earned LockCoins to LOCK tokens anytime using the in-app swap feature. LOCK tokens can be used for premium features, governance voting, and can be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges.

How do AI assistants help me? +

Our AI assistants help in multiple ways: suggesting smart replies to conversations, optimizing your content for better engagement, monitoring for safety and inappropriate content, and providing personalized recommendations. All AI processing respects your privacy and data.

Can I use LockGram with my existing Telegram account? +

Yes, LockGram integrates seamlessly with your existing Telegram account. You can use your current username and contacts. LockGram works as an extension of Telegram, so all your existing Telegram features remain available.

Is there a cost to use LockGram? +

LockGram is free to use! You can earn LockCoins through normal usage, invite friends, and create content without any cost. Premium features and advanced functionality may require LOCK tokens, but the core social features remain free forever.
