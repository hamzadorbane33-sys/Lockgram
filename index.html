Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LockGram

What is LockGram?

+

LockGram is a Web3 social application that runs as a Telegram Mini-App. It combines traditional social networking with cryptocurrency rewards, end-to-end encryption, and AI-powered features. Users earn LockCoins for interactions and enjoy privacy-first social experiences.

How do I earn LockCoins?

+

You earn LockCoins through various activities: posting content, engaging with others' posts, referring friends, participating in community events, and maintaining an active, positive presence. The more you contribute to the community, the more you earn!

Is LockGram really secure?

+

Yes! LockGram uses end-to-end encryption (AES-256) for all messages and implements zero-knowledge architecture. This means only you and your intended recipients can read your messages‚Äînot even LockGram can access them. Our security has been audited by independent firms.

Do I need a crypto wallet?

+

Not immediately! You can start using LockGram without a wallet and earn LockCoins. However, to withdraw or trade your LockCoins, you'll need to connect a crypto wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet. We support all major wallets.

How is this different from regular social media?

+

Unlike traditional social media, LockGram: 1) Pays you for your content and engagement with LockCoins, 2) Provides end-to-end encryption for true privacy, 3) Uses Web3 technology for decentralization and ownership, 4) Has no ads or data selling, and 5) Features AI assistants to enhance your experience.

Is it free to use?

+

Absolutely! LockGram is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees, no premium tiers, and no hidden costs. You can start earning LockCoins immediately after joining, and all core features are available to everyone.

What can I do with LockCoins?

+

LockCoins can be used for: premium features, tipping other users, accessing exclusive content, participating in governance votes, trading on supported exchanges, or holding as an investment. The utility of LockCoins continues to expand with new features.

How do I get started?

+

Simply search for "@LockGramBot" in Telegram, click "Start," and the Mini-App will launch. Follow the onboarding process, and you'll be earning LockCoins within minutes! No downloads, no complicated setup‚Äîjust pure Web3 social experience.
